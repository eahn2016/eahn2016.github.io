Introduction

"I Died on a Full Stomach" is a short platformer game that I created for the Game Boy. It has two levels and ends with a boss fight. Everything except for the audio and start, title, win, and lose screens' art is created by me.

Synopsis

You are a ghost who is in need of a toilet. In order to reach the toilet, you must go through the underworld's challenges and weave through many devils who attempt to thwart your journey, while collecting toilet paper. You can poop on top of the heads of devils in order to kill them. The second level includes a boss.
